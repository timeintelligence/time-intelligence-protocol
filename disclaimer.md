This protocol is a thinking tool, not a recommendation engine.

Outputs generated from the protocol are conditional analytical artifacts. They depend on the supplied birth data, chart data, Luck Pillar table, annual data, assumptions, and execution discipline.

The protocol does not establish certainty. It does not predict guaranteed outcomes. It does not provide commands.
